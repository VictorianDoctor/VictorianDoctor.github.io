Midnight Cure

For The Queens!

Your fellow Brothers and Sisters were ordered to build a ship-killer platform because of a nearby corpus outpost. Their ships may have been bigger and more advanced, but any ship can be taken down with enough artillery.

Today, however, you drew the short stick: You get put on crew ship duty. It's much less work, but that's the issue: It's boring. As you sit below deck, taking inventory, you hear a shout from one of your Brothers... It's the Tenno.

You grab your Grakata and run to help your Brothers. The Tenno was powerful, but they couldn't take you all, Right?

But as you reach the top of the stairs, you finally hear it: The Alarms, They destroyed the reactor. Looking around, you also see the bodies of your Brother's and Sister's around you: They didn't stand a chance.

You had one chance. But in the end, you know it wouldn't have made a difference. Plus, you finally realize something. It all meant nothing, your entire life: Everything you've done. You will just get replaced by clones and crossed off a list. You shed a tear then and mutter your final words: "For the Queens." The words mean nothing anymore, but it's all you've ever known. You then sigh your final breath.
