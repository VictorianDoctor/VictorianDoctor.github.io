About the Author

Athyes is a reserved and introspective individual who finds solace in personal growth and self-improvement. As a plague doctor, Athyes is immensely intrigued by exploring medical research, particularly diseases that have captured his interest. Proficient in Latin, he enjoys delving into the historical roots of his field. In his spare time, Athyes embraces historical practices of Victorian monks, such as baking bread and meditation, finding peace and fulfillment in these activities.
