About the Author

Athyes is a reserved individual who dedicates much of his time to bettering himself and those around him socially, emotionally, and mentally, but as a plague doctor, sometimes also physically.

From a young age, Athyes found interest in video games, horror, engineering, and a notable fascination with disease. Due to his love of disease and horror, he would become completely enthralled with the concept of plague doctors and, thus, Victorian monks.

Athyes, armed with an obsession with medieval history, monks, and plague doctors, applied the practices of Victorian Monks to his day-to-day life, Teaching himself how to bake, meditate, and even Latin.

Nowadays, Athyes spends most of his day doing research, gaming, or creating fiction worlds. He doesn't find himself interesting, but you appear to since you made it this far, so feel free to stick around.
