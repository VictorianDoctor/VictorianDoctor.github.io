About the Author

So you want to know a bit about the Cap'n, Huh? Well... I suppose it's only fair.

Ahoy, My name is Cord and online I go by The Lootificer, a username I came up with to showcase my love for all things Pirate and Steampunk while also leaning into my passion for Dungeons and Dragons.

I've enjoyed writing and story-telling since I was a wee lad, and so one day when I got bored I decided to make a website to share my stories with well... You.
