NEW KILLER – THE SCOURGE

A notorious doctor with a reputable name. But as decades passed, his methods got crueler, Leaving countless lives lost to his hands. His now tarnished reputation leaves fear in the hearts around him.

Malpractice

The Scourge doesn't have an active ability, he instead gains affects Survivors overtime with slower action speed while gaining speed and terror radius.

Special Status Effect – Psychosis: Psychosis punishes Survivors who spend too much time alone. Survivors afflicted with Psychosis perform Conspicuous Actions more slowly overall. To recover from Psychosis, the affected Survivor must stay close to other Survivors.
