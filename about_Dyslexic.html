Midnight Cure Apothecary

About this site

Midnight Cure Apothecary was founded in August of 1856 by Dr. Athyes Gallus Woods after a tragic fire that claimed no casualties except for a young boy named Harlow Ward, whom the doctor had adopted a year prior.

Local police suspected foul play, attributing the fire to rival local doctors aiming to eliminate competition. Rumors also circulated about Dr. Woods' unusual height, standing at an impressive seven feet tall. Before the incident, the apothecary operated from Dr. Woods' childhood home, a place with deep family roots.

After the fire, Dr. Woods disappeared for two years, only to reappear transformed. What was once a modest home now stands as a majestic stone tower, housing a clinic and apothecary. The local medical community is abuzz with speculation. Updates on this intriguing transformation are forthcoming.
